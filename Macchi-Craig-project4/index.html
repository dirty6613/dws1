ABOUT

DJ Spinnerz is a world famous DJ. Known globally for packing clubs and setting parties to a new standard. DJ Spinnerz started Deejaying in his basement at 13. He would spin records for hours with his friends. He started doing school dances and instantly fell in love with his ability to move the crowd.

J. Johnson says:
He is the best DJ ever...PERIOD!

Well versed in music DJ Spinnerz spins everything from:

  • Hip Hop
  • Club
  • Pop
  • R & B
  • Break-Beats
  • Drum & Bass
  • Anything you want!

DJ Spinnerz has opened and toured with infamous acts, such as:
  1. The Other Guys
  2. Your Mamma's Favorite Rapper
  3. Dem Peeps
  4. Otis Jones
  5. Ricky Rick & The Richards
